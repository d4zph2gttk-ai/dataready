FAQ

Before you clean an Excel or CSV file.

Does this replace human review?

No. DataReady is a time-saving cleanup tool. It helps standardize files and flag risky rows, but important data should still be verified by a human.

Do you save uploaded data?

No. Files are processed locally in your browser and are not saved by DataReady.

What files work best?

Customer lists, CRM exports, contact lists, rosters, invoices, and raw CSV files.

What do I download?

A cleaned CSV file and a PDF data-cleaning report with metrics, issues, and top changes.

Can I send feedback?

Yes. DataReady is actively improving, and customer suggestions help shape better cleanup rules for real-world files.
